Loan EMI Comparison

  • Personal Loan EMI: Higher interest, shorter tenure
  • Home Loan EMI: Lower interest, longer tenure
  • Car Loan EMI: Medium interest, fixed tenure

How EMI and Interest Work

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) depends on loan amount, interest rate, and tenure. A lower interest rate reduces total payable amount, while longer tenure reduces EMI but increases total interest.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is EMI?

EMI is the fixed monthly payment made to repay a loan.

How is EMI calculated?

EMI is calculated using loan amount, interest rate, and tenure.

Which loan has lowest EMI?

Home loans usually have the lowest EMI due to lower interest rates.

Can EMI change?

Yes, if interest rate is floating, EMI may change.
